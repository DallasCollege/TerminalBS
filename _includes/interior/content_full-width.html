Who is Josh Lyman?

Josh Lyman is a highly regarded political strategist with an impressive resume:

  • Graduated cum laude from Harvard University and earned a law degree from Yale Law School.
  • Chief of Staff for President Matt Santos.
  • Deputy Chief of Staff for President Jed Bartlet.
  • Campaign Manager for Santos/McGarry for a Brighter America.
  • Legislative Director in the House of Representatives.
  • Floor Manager to the House Minority Whip.

Lyman is well known for his sharp political mind, strategic thinking, and ability to navigate complex political landscapes. He has also played a key role in significant legislative victories and was instrumental in the presidential campaigns of John Hoynes, Jed Bartlet and Matthew Santos.
