Overview

The Organic Gardening program offers students a comprehensive understanding of sustainable gardening practices. The curriculum covers soil health, composting, pest management, and plant selection. Through hands-on projects and workshops, students learn to grow their own organic produce, promoting a healthier lifestyle and environmental stewardship. This program prepares students to create and maintain their own organic gardens, fostering a deeper connection with nature.
