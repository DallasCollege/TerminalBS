Dallas College Student Email

Use Dallas College Student Email (Microsoft Outlook) to communicate with other students, faculty, staff and anyone else who has an email address. It's free for students.

And as of Dec. 2022, it's the official way Dallas College will communicate with you.

Once you're registered for classes you can log in with your Dallas College username and password and start using Dallas College Student Email right away.

Access your Dallas College student email:

  • outlook.dcccd.edu
  • the email link at the top of dallascollege.edu
  • SharePoint
