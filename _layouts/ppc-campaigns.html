Why Dallas College?

 

Free Learning Materials

The cost of most required materials—including textbooks, software, and lab kits—is covered by your tuition, saving you hundreds of dollars each semester.

Incredible
Value

Dallas College offers one of the lowest tuition rates in the state, and provides free student services such as food pantries, health centers, counseling, tutoring and career advising.

Convenient Locations

With multiple campuses across Dallas County and online courses, there’s a Dallas College location near you. Free parking and public transit passes are available to simplify your commute.

Flexible
Classes

Choose between traditional and accelerated courses, plus classes that take place on weekends, in the evenings or during non-traditional times like the winter holidays.
