Degree Plan Code: C1.DAD.ANIMATION

The Animation Certificate emphasizes individualized real-world, hands-on technical and conceptual skills for students to incorporate a specialized focus of animation. The certificate provides students with entry- and intermediate-level skills in the development of motion graphics, 2-D and 3-D animations, 2-D and 3-D modeling and rendering, and digital video.

Semester 1

  • ARTC 1302 - Digital Imaging I (3 Credit Hours)
  • ARTC 1305 - Basic Graphic Design (3 Credit Hours)
  • ARTV 1303 - Basic Animation (3 Credit Hours)
  • ARTS 1316 - Drawing I (3 Credit Hours)

Semester Total: 12 Credit Hours

Semester 2

  • ARTV 1345 - 3-D Modeling and Rendering I (3 Credit Hours)
  • ARTV 2301 - 2-D Animation I (3 Credit Hours)
  • ARTV 1341 - 3-D Animation I (3 Credit Hours)

Semester Total: 9 Credit Hours

Semester 3

  • ARTV 2345 - 3-D Modeling and Rendering II (3 Credit Hours)

  • OR

  • ARTV 2351 - 3-D Animation II (3 Credit Hours)

  •  

  • ARTV 2330 - 2-D Animation II (3 Credit Hours)
  • ARTV 1351 - Digital Video (3 Credit Hours)

Semester Total: 9 Credit Hours

Minimum Hours Required: 30 Credit Hours
